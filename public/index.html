Why we built this

From Bengaluru, with stubbornness.

UNFOLD was built in Bengaluru by an engineer who got tired of watching good people get reduced to thumbnails — including himself. Years of swipe apps taught us one thing: attraction triggered by a photo fades; attraction earned through conversation compounds.

So we flipped the order. Words first. Face later. The glass in between isn't a gimmick — it's a promise that someone chose your sentences before they ever saw your smile.

Tonight is our first live test, and you're not an audience — you're a co-designer. Every message you send, every cap you hit, every piece of feedback you leave decides what UNFOLD becomes.
