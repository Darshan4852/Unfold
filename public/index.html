08Why we built this

From Bengaluru,
with stubbornness.

UNFOLD was built by an engineer who got tired of watching good people get reduced to thumbnails — including himself. Years of swipe apps taught us one thing: attraction triggered by a photo fades; attraction earned through conversation compounds.

So we flipped the order. Words first. Face later. The glass in between isn't a gimmick — it's a promise that someone chose your sentences before they ever saw your smile.
